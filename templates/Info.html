Problem Statement

Clinical decisions are often made based on doctorsâ€™ experience and intuition rather than on the knowledge-rich data hidden in the data. This leads to errors and high costs that affect the quality of the medical services. The use of analytic tools and data modelling can aid in the improvement of clinical decisions. Thus, the goal here is to build a web application to help doctors diagnose diabetes using a web browser.


Feature

We collected the diabetets dataset from UCI ML. The dataset has the following 5 attributes:

Age: Age in years.
BMI: BMI stand for Body Mass Index.
Insulin: Controls the level of glucose in body,
Diabes PF: Related to your family history.
Glucose: Level of glucose in mg/dl.


Result

We are able to predict whether the person has Diabetes or not. It will be helpful in remote places to mass test with only few machnies to classify people for further awareness.
